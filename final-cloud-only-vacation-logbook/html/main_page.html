Your Very Personalized Vacation Logbook

Whether it's going to be Summer or Winter, it's time to start planning your vacation! Our vacation logbook is here to help you with that. Powered by Google Places, you'll be able to search up places to go and things to do in no time, and save lists of such places so that you don't forget them!

Note: this is primararily a server backend that serves the APIs for your account. Authenticating below creates a unique account in our database, and redirects you to a simple frontend that shows a list of places you've added associated with your account.

You can use our APIs in order to add Places to User Accounts. A frontend application is not being served by this backend at this time.
